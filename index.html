Blood Pressure and Radial Pulse

In this activity, you will be provided with an opportunity to take
the blood pressure and radial pulse of a person in various states of exertion.


Each test will provide you with:


  • a stopwatch, or sphygmomanometer
  • visual and aural queues to help monitor heartrate, and systolic/diastolic pressure
  • a multiple choice question to record your results

Recommendations:


  • It is strongly recommended to complete this activity in Chrome.
  • Record your preliminary results on a sheet of paper.
  • Choose the closest possible answer from the list.
  • When taking the heartrate, use pencil and paper to mark each beat for 15 seconds.
  • Don't forget to multiply your heartrate by four. (beats in fifteen seconds)*4
